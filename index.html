Save the Animals!

My name is Maddie Carroll, and my passion is to help animals in need. Through this webpage I hope to bring consumers the information they need in order to take a stand against product testing on animals. Check out my blog posts, podcast, and short video made by myself and some of my peers at Virginia Tech!

Blog Posts

  • Humans to Animals

    Go ahead and read my first post, which serves as an introduction to my cause and addresses why it must be discussed.

  • Adopt-Don't-Shop

    My second post focuses on the key idea in the video, Adopt-Don't-Shop. Adopting can save a life that could have been selected for testing.

  • Companies without Morals

    This post calls out the large companies that continue to test on animals instead of trying other methods that are proven to be more cost effective and more humane.

  • Medical and Product Testing

    Take a look at my final post which discusses the history of scientific as well as medical testing in the United States and around the world. There is also emphasis on new testing methods that could eradicate the old outdated methods such as testing on animals.
